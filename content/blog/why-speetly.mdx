# Welcome to the Speetly blog

We are happy to welcome you on the official Speetly blog, your freelance project management platform. In this first article, we want to present our vision and how Speetly can help you succeed as a freelancer.

## Why Speetly ?

As freelancers, we know how difficult it can be to juggle between project management, payment tracking and communication with clients. That's why we created Speetly, a one-stop solution that simplifies your daily life.

### Our main features

1. **Intuitive project management**
   - Custom dashboard
   - Deadline tracking
   - Task organization

2. **Payment tracking**
   - Simplified invoicing
   - Automatic reminders
   - Transaction history

3. **Professional client interface**
   - Dedicated client space
   - Document sharing
   - Centralized communication
