# Privacy Policy

## Introduction

At Speetly, we place a high priority on protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information.

## Data Collected

### 1. Subscription Information

We collect the following information when you sign up:
- Name and surname
- Professional email address
- Phone number
- Billing information

### 2. Usage Data

We also collect:
- Login data
- Interactions with the platform
- User preferences

## Usage of Data

Your data is used for:

1. **Providing our services**
   - Managing your account
   - Processing payments
   - Customer support

2. **Improve our platform**
   - Analyse of performances
   - Development of new features
   - Personalization of the user experience

## Data Protection

### Security Measures

We implement the following measures:
- Encryption of sensitive data
- Advanced security protocols
- Restricted access to data

### Hosting

Your data is hosted in the European Union, in accordance with the GDPR.

## Your Rights

According to the GDPR, you have the following rights:

- Right to access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to portability
- Right to object
- Right to limitation of processing

## Cookies

Our site uses cookies to:
- Improve navigation
- Analyze site usage
- Personalize your experience

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time.

## Data Retention

We retain your data:
- During the duration of your subscription
- 3 years after your last connection
- According to legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We share your data with:
- Our technical subcontractors
- Authorities (upon request)
- Payment services

## Modifications

We reserve the right to modify this policy. Users will be notified of significant changes.
